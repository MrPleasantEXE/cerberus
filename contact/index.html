How to contact Cerberus

We can't always be around for you, especially in-game, sometimes you need to contact us outside of the game, which is fine! So below you will see different ways to contact Cerberus, but please be aware we cannot respond right away! The best way possible is we suggest using Discord.

Email

Contacting us via email is the slowest method, however, we will still get back to you... please give us up to 48 hours to respond, our email is support@cerberusguild.com

Discord

Using Discord is the best way to contact us, we are usually on there quite a lot and we have it on our mobile for outside of the game purpose, to join simply click me!

Teamspeak

We have a Teamspeak server which you are free to join, there is a dedicated tech support room if you need any help, you can join by clicking me! or use ts3.pleasantgaming.com

In-game

If there are any staff members online we will always help our members if needed, just send a message in game and we will try our best to resolve any issues or assist you in any way
