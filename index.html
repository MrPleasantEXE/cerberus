About

When Creating Cerberus our idea was simple, we wanted to make a guild that was fun, a guild where there were no real requirements to join or demands (such as 400ap/dp), we wanted to let every member experience all aspects of Black Desert without having to sacrifice having fun (such as life skills, self-improvement and PVP), most guilds do not act like this, we left Lazarus for that very reason and created Cerberus. We focus on Life Skills, self-improvement and PVP (such as Node Wars and training). We have many players from different places around the world! Such as Russia, Greece, Spain, Germany and lots more! Cerberus has now become a community and is currently expanding! We are not just 1 guild anymore and we hope to keep expanding in the near future.
